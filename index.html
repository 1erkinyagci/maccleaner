FAQ

Is MacCleaner safe?

Yes. It never deletes anything automatically, moves files to Trash by default, and protects system-critical paths.

Does MacCleaner delete files automatically?

No. You review and confirm every cleanup. Nothing is removed without your approval.

Is this a RAM cleaner?

No. MacCleaner frees disk space — it doesn't make false performance or "memory boost" claims.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Scanning is local-first and runs entirely on your Mac.

What can I clean for free?

Unlimited scans plus up to 500 MB per cleanup of cache, logs, trash, and old downloads.

Why is System Data so large on my Mac?

It's mostly caches, logs, and leftovers that accumulate over time — exactly what MacCleaner helps you review and clear.

Can I restore deleted files?

Yes. Files moved to Trash can be restored from Finder anytime.

Does MacCleaner upload my files?

No. File names and paths never leave your Mac unless you explicitly contact support.
